custom_levels(*levels)

Sets the custom levels. This method accepts symbols or strings. 

  Configurator.custom_levels('My', 'Custom', :Levels)

Alternatively, you can specify custom levels in XML: 

  <log4r_config>
    <pre_config>
      <custom_levels>
        My, Custom, Levels
      </custom_levels>
      ...
load_xml_file(filename)
